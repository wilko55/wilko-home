Mentor Match

A mentor matching platform for civil servants. This started as a side project but quickly grew to almost 4000 users. It has dominated much of my free time for the past year.

As the sole developer, running a live service alongside my day job has been interesting. We've recently undergone a penetration test and
are about to become the recommended mentoring platform for the UK civil service.

HMPO passport renewal

HMPO has released a web app which allows users to renew their UK passports online.

I've been working on adding features to the site, including the overseas advice pages and filter. This is an ExpressJS app using the HMPO forms framework.
I've thoroughly enjoyed working in a multi-dispiplinary agile team, collaborating closely with user researchers, content designers and business analysts.
